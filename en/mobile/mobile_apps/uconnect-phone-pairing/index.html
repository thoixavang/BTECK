UCONNECT® PHONE PAIRING SITE

Go to Site

This site helps you pair your Bluetooth®-enabled phone to your Uconnect® system quickly and easily. All you need to do is select your vehicle, select your Uconnect radio and select your mobile phone. The site will then provide you with:

  • A list of calling & texting capabilities, music features and pairing characteristics
  • Step-by-step instructions, operating both your Uconnect system and your mobile phone
  • Print capability, so you can take a hard copy of the instructions with you
